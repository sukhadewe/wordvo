WORDVO

Free Daily Word Puzzle

Wordvo is a free, minimalist daily word puzzle. Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 tries — a new word every day.

Features

  • 🟩 Daily word challenge + unlimited archive
  • ⚡ Speed Mode — race against the clock
  • 👥 Multiplayer — 2 players on one device
  • 🎓 Practice Mode — unlimited attempts
  • 🏆 Achievements & streak tracking
  • 💡 Hint system (up to 2 per game)
  • 🎨 Custom color themes (Ocean, Sunset, Midnight)
  • ♿ Colorblind mode
  • 🔊 Keyboard sound themes
  • 📱 Installable as a PWA

Built By

Word list from open-source English dictionaries. Inspired by the original Wordle by Josh Wardle.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data We Collect

Wordvo does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game runs entirely in your browser.

Local Storage

Your stats, streak, settings, and achievements are saved locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Wordvo loads fonts from Google Fonts and styles from Tailwind CSS CDN. These services may log your IP as part of standard server logs.

Cookies & Analytics

We do not use cookies or any analytics or tracking tools. No gameplay data is sent to any server.
